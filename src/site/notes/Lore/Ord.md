# Description
- The Unbending, the Wise, the Secret-Keeper
- God of Magic, Knowledge, Secrets, and Equilibrium
- Maxims of Ord:
	- 1st: 
		- Ord understands the totality of possible knowledge that has ever, currently, or could ever exist
	- 2nd: 
		- Knowledge is power and power must be kept in equilibrium.
	- 3rd:
		- Magic is the offspring of knowledge and intention
	- 4th: 
		- Know Thyself
