## Description
- An ancient kingdom that used to inhabit the forest now known as [[Locations/Gloomwood Locations/The Gloomwood\|The Gloomwood]]
- The kingdom was attacked by the [[Lore/Didachian Hegemony\|Didachian Hegemony]] during its conquests and was eventually defeated in a strange event that left their woods cloaked in dark magic.
- In the [[Lore/Fourth Age\|Fourth Age]], the region of [[Locations/Old Lystria\|Old Lystria]] takes its name from the fallen kingdom
