#Region
# Description
- A sprawling hinterland of prairie and savannah that extends to cliffs of the [[Locations/Djurum\|Djurum]]
- Agriculturally fertile, Old Lystria was settled by farmer colonists (mostly humans and halflings) by the [[Lore/Didachian Hegemony\|Didachian Hegemony]] after the [[Lore/Lystrian Crusade\|Lystrian Crusade]]
- [[Locations/Gorm's Eye\|Gorm's Eye]] began as a frontier barracks and grew into the largest town and trade hub in the region.
