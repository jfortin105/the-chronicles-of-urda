# Description
- The Djurum is a vast wasteland of cruel elements and desperate mortals. 
- Tales tell that the Djurum was once an ancient great lake that was drained by a great calamity, resulting in unique and unhospitable geography. 
- Great cliffs ring the northern approaches, acting as a tamper on great storms that break upon the rock face. 
- Sandstorms are common and dramatically alter the landscape with each raging event. The is as dangerous, perilously beating down upon the open landscape
- The desolate seabed has formed ravines, escarpments, plateaus, and other rocky projections that disrupt the seemingly endless horizon of dunes.
- Resources are scarce and are the source of conflict for the limited mortal population that make up the desert
