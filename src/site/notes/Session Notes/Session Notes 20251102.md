# Intro - Mechanics Going Forward
- 1 gp = 10sp
- Unarmed combat = 1d2
- Luck tokens --> Get one each session
	- NPCs
		- You can give a luck token to an NPC to use in the moment of a failed role, if they Positively or Neutrally aligned to Party.
- Rations 
	- To rest, a character must consume a ration and sleep for 8 hours
		- Sleep can be broken up for light and routine tasks, such as taking a turn on watch
	- PC can forage for 1 ration per day with an INT check
	- PC can travel and sleep without consuming a ration, but take an exhaustion penalty after not eating for 3 days
- Travel time
	- PCs can travel up to 8 hours a day
		- Pass increasing CON checks to push further
	- hexes are 6 miles

| Method            | Time      |
| ----------------- | --------- |
| Walking           | 4 hrs     |
| Mounted           | 2 hrs     |
| Sailing           | 1 hrs     |
| Difficult Terrain | 2x normal |
| Arduous Terrain   | 8 hours   |
