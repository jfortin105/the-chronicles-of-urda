# Carousing
- When you return from your adventures, you can use [[Mechanics and Rules/Downtime\|Downtime]] to carouse and celebrate your heroic exploits
- To carouse, each participant pitches in for the cost of the event and makes a "Carousing Roll"
	- See "Event Costs and Bonuses" below


# Carousing Roll
- Each participant makes a Carousing Roll to determine their individual actions and outcomes during the carousing, which includes gaining XP
	- 1d8 + event bonus (+ bard bonus if applicable)

# Experience Gain
- XP is determined by your individual outcome and applicable roll
- XP from each individual outcome is pooled and then distributed evenly across the party
