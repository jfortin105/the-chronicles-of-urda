# Resting
- Injured characters can rest to recover from their wounds
- To rest, a character must consume a ration and sleep for 8 hours
	- Sleep can broken up for light and routine tasks, such as taking a turn on watch

# Success
- A character who successfully rests regains all lost HP and recovers any stat damage
	- Additionally, some talents, spells or items regain their ability to be used after a successful rest

# Interruption
- Each stressful interruption of rest (including combat) requires an affected character to make a DC 12 CON check
- On a failure, the character consumes a ration but gains no benefit from resting
