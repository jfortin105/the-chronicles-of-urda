# Item
- +1 Staff
# Possession
- 2212-X
# Benefit
- All snakes regard you with a friendly attitude unless you do something to upset them. 
- Once per long rest, it becomes a giant snake for 5 rounds that obeys your mental commands. If the giant snake goes to 0 HP, it reverts into a staff.
# Curse
- You have disadvantage on attacks and casting hostile spells targeting snakes.
